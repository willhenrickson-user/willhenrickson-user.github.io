I’m an Information Scientist specializing in data visualization and reporting

I believe that the most powerful aspect of data is the ability to turn numbers into stories. Data and statistics are the numeric proof of what happens around the world at any given time, whether it be human activity, natural phenomenon, sales, trends, rising, falling, its the absolute evidence of what we may or may not already perceive yet cannot claim to be entirely true. Having studied and the University of Colorado Boulder and currently working as an ERP Systems Intern at Kodiak Building Partners, my interest in learning and turning the abstract into the understandable is what I love most in this field, and what I would love to continue to do going forward.



I’m currently seeking a role in the field of data analysis and reporting, as I feel that turning scattered data into concise visualizations for others to make inferences from and understand is the most interesting aspect of the field of Information Science as a whole.


I’m from Castle Rock, Colorado originally, and my interests include drawing, snowboarding, and spending time with friends. The ability to combine statistics and computing with art is what drew me into the field in the first place, and I believe that data visualization is a great merger of those interests and disciplines.
